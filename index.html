The Jobs That I Have Done

I like this job because it was my first job and wasn't too difficult, but it got me out therem and allowed me to get more accustomed to many different people. It is always fun to see people still there that I worked with whenever me and my family eat at subway.

Kanakuk Kamps

My second job was to be a camp couselor at a Christian Sports camp called Kanakuk. They are based in Branson, MO and I was in one of their seven camps called K-Kountry. It is the runt of all the camps for its size, and because we have the youngest kids ranging from 6-11 years old. This job has been probably my favorite.

Math Tutor

This is the job that I currently am doing now. This is my second semester doing it. I never thought I would be good at helping others in math because I thought I couldn't explain it well. However it turns out that I am actually pretty decent at teaching others math and am requested a lot to help people privately through the support center through the school. I am decent at math, but there are other people that work with me that are far better than I am. I am very good with people and even if I don't know something I can always learn and find out. This job has been maybe the most rewarding in the sense that when people tell me they did better on their test because they came to get help.
